A bit about me

I moved to the United States of America eight years ago from Latvia - a small, but beautiful country located in Northern Europe. I came to the PNW to study and learn amore about the American culure. My original plan was to go back home after I'm done with school; however, life happens. I met my husband here and now I'm planning on staying here in US. Even though all my family is still living in Latvia, I get to visit them every summer and thanks to the technology, I get to speak to them almost every day using Skype or other mobile applications.

Currently I am a student at Eastern Washington University majoring in Marketing and Visual Communication Design. I am very happy with my chosen field. I highly enjoy the business aspect of marketing and the problem solving, creating aspect of design. I know that I still have a lot to learn, but I'm happy with the knowledge I have gained so far, and I am even more excited about the opportunities the future holds.
